Chalk Climbing Centre

At Chalk Climbing Centre, we prioritize the safety and well-being of our climbers. That's why we have implemented a mandatory induction process for all first-time climbers. Our dedicated staff members are committed to providing a thorough and comprehensive training session before climbers are allowed to scale our indoor rock climbing walls. During the induction, our experienced instructors cover essential topics such as equipment usage, proper harness fitting, belaying techniques, and the correct way to tie knots. We ensure that each climber understands the fundamental safety procedures and protocols before they begin their climbing journey. This mandatory induction helps create a safe and supportive environment for all climbers, ensuring that everyone is well-prepared and confident in their abilities. So, whether you're a beginner or an experienced climber, our induction process is designed to ensure a secure and enjoyable experience for all at Chalk Climbing Centre.
